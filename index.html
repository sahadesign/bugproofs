What is Kickstart?

Kickstart is a website to advertise, guide and aid startups to evolve so as to become successful ventures. It serves as a platform to showcase various oppertunities among each domain. Using this information users could even get their startups registered in the website for other users to view and progress accordingly.

This website is aimed at assimilating the information on various startups that are blooming up in the present markets and thus virtually provide a relatable atmosphere for other ventures to view, thereby evolve accordingly. By collecting data of new startups we could provide new options to the users with respect to a particular domain.
